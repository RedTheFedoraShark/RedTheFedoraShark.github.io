Interactive calendar for Blades in the Dark

About

Blades in the Dark is an RPG system by John Harper. It comes with it's own calendar system. This interactive calendar was made for the purpose of recording events during my campaign in the game.

The Calendar

Year in BitD is split into 6 months, each split into 10 weeks. A week has 6 days, giving a 360 day year with 60 day months. Around Doskvol there are three seasons: cold winter (Mendar and Elisar), rainy spring (Kalivet and Suran) and stormy autumn (Ulsivet and Volvinet). Read more.

How to use

To see any journal entry left-click on a week box. To deselct and come back to this welcome message simply right-click anywhere or click "about page" button at the top.
