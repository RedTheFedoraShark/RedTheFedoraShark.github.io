Red's Logpad. Entry 12-01-79

Took me a while to find this old dusty thing. Thought I lost it forever... just like sailing the seas. It was a much needed break, but... eh I am not the sedentary type. Some folks can stay in one place their whole life, for me half a year's too much. Left the old ship on good terms with the crew, they left me in corrupted City, with a few pennies and a letter of recommendation. Too bad the security laughed at it, most of them anyway. Had enough saved to get meself a neat little apartment though.

An older guy came by the next day, sailor I think. He had a lot to say and a lot to ask. Local seccies were nothing more than milksops. Private detective - sounded so serious, but I liked it. Be my own boss, deal with problems my way. There was plenty of work too. But now, am done. Some see a friend in me, some tried to court me. Tiring. Reading through the old entries brings back memories... I should come by the dock someday, maybe someone's looking for a sailor.
