Red's Logpad. Entry 25-03-77

Nome Facility was a nice place. Plenty of room, no nerds. Military kind of place. Had some salary to spend. Cap said we leaving soon, but I managed to get us some steel and shit in time. Now time to salvage some wrecks.

Coalition wanted to figure out what's happened to their sub. Spent a whole night searching that thing - big ship. Crawlers ate them off guard I guess. Didn't read the logbooks, but found three of them buggers on board, plenty of unused ammo and a crew. Massacred - gave me some real creeps. Time for a nap.

Fuck. Had an hour nap interrupted by an unmarked beacon. Made redundant? Who knows, but spent too much time staring down the sights to take out just two buggers. Not even worth the effort, we found nothing there. I hope cap won't find me anythin to do now.

We got to that research place. Plenty of nerds, studying buggers. They had another problem with the cages, all the security had to rush in. Coalition should invest in better cages. Not much to buy from the nerds. A bunch of medicine, useful but it's no lead. But the overseer had some good news. Some Pirate lord further south. 5000 marks for their heads. Good few hours of sleep and we were on the hunt.

Locked myself in, sat down, checked the ammo. Well, maybe the other way round. Coil crew saw them first. They punched a few holes and sent them sinking. Then I made a nice hole in their plating and dumped some c-4 shell right into their reactor. On accident I blew up half their ship. Only realised what I hit once on board that thing. One engie blockhead survived. Locked himself in crew quarters, smart man. A shame he had a bounty on his head. Unloaded half a mag on him. Hope he felt every one of these. And that was another night of looting. Now an hour off. I need a drink.

Maybe a mile further a beacon was down. Empty one this time so we focused on maintanance. Almost an entire day repairing that old piece of steel. Barely anythig was working after that pirate fight. Planted some new trees in the garden. Gonna make some wine with the fruits. Now time for some sleep.
